Why am I here, at Coding Temple?

I was drawn to Coding Temple because it felt like the perfect
match for my journey to become a certified software engineer.
Balancing the demanding schedule of a professional firefighter
—24 hours on and 48 hours off—is no small feat. The remote
learning setup at Coding Temple, tailored to the unpredictability
of my hours, provides the flexibility I need to pursue coding without
compromising my commitment to firefighting. It's not just about
the program; it's about finding a supportive community that
understands the unique challenges of my profession. Coding Temple
is more than a bootcamp; it's the ideal partner for my career transition.
